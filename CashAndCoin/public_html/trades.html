Trades

For individuals that would like to trade personally with me for specific coins and banknotes must read the How to offer a trade section of the page. Please read the policy section underneath so that you know what to expect when requesting to trade with me.

Policy:

Before you offer a trade please read this IMPORTANT information:
  • I am very serious about trading

  • I do not accept fake or damaged items

  • The coin or banknotes which you wish to pay for are the final prices (This mean no haggling)

  • Please be mindful of the items which I have currently displayed in Collection, I will not trade previously sold items

How to offer a trade:

If you want to offer a trade you must reach out to me on the Contact page. The following steps are meant to act as a guideline for how the information should be sent:
  1. Enter your name and age. Please not ages under 18 will not be accepted to trade.

  2. Enter the address or location you wish to receive the package.

  3. Enter an e-mail and phone umber that can be reached out to you. I recommend that you use Gmail or Hotmail however, any other form of other emails should be fine.

  4. Enter the specific item you wish to trade for.

  5. Check over all the information and then proceed to click the submit button to finish.

What steps are next:

If you have been successful with submitting all the required information you will receive an email with a list of instructions for the trade otherwise, you will receive a denial email listing the problem that has been encountered with the trade. Please noteif you wrote an email without following the steps above it will not be ignored
